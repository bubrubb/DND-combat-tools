🎭 PLINNY'S BARD COMBAT CHECKLIST 🎵

📖 Need help with spell selection and tactics? Check the Strategy Guide

🎯 Phase 1: Pre-Combat Assessment

Count Enemies & Allies
How many enemies? Are they clustered or spread? Where are my allies?
Check Resources
Bardic Inspiration uses (3 total), spell slots (4/3/2), HP status
Identify Priority Targets
Spellcasters, ranged attackers, or wounded enemies to finish off?
Position Assessment
Am I in melee range? Do I need to move? Can I stay at range safely?

⚡ Phase 2: Action Decision

Choose Primary Action
SPELL: AoE control/damage, single target save-or-suck
ATTACK: Consistent damage with Blade Flourish potential
SUPPORT: Bardic Inspiration for crucial ally moments
If Casting: Choose Spell Level
3rd: Hypnotic Pattern (AoE control)
2nd: Suggestion, Blindness/Deafness
1st: Faerie Fire, Hideous Laughter, Healing Word

🎲 Phase 3: Execute Action

If Attacking: Roll & Apply Dueling
Rapier: d20 + 6 to hit, 1d8 + 5 damage
Hand Crossbow: d20 + 6 to hit, 1d6 + 3 damage
If Hit: Consider Blade Flourish
Defensive: +AC until next turn
Slashing: Hit another enemy within 5ft
Mobile: Push target + move with them
If Casting: Apply Spell Effects
Spell Save DC: 14
Spell Attack: d20 + 6
Remember concentration spells!

🎵 Phase 4: Bonus Actions & Support

Bardic Inspiration Available?
Give to ally making important attack, save, or skill check (d6 bonus die)
Healing Word if Needed
Bonus action to revive downed ally: 1d4+3 healing at range
Movement & Positioning
25ft speed - stay at optimal range, use Halfling Nimbleness to move through larger enemies

⚔️ Phase 5: Combat Reactions

Opportunity Attacks
Rapier +6 for 1d8+5 damage when enemies leave my reach
Lucky Racial Feature
If I roll a 1 on attack, ability check, or save - reroll and use new result
Concentration Saves
When taking damage while concentrating: Constitution save DC 10 or half damage
